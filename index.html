Asset 01
Personal Brand Script — Career Profiling Campaign
Client: Shimaa (Career Coach) · Platform: Short-form video · Language: Arabic · ABZ Content
Script A — Variant 1
أصعب إحساس في الشغل مش إنك تكون مضغوط. أصعب إحساس إنك تكون بتجري بكل سرعتك بس في الاتجاه الغلط. سنين بنحاول نغيّر نفسنا عشان نليق على مكان. بدل ما ندور على المكان اللي يليق على طبيعتنا. الحقيقة إن المشكلة مش في قدراتك. المشكلة إنك لسه ما فهمتش نفسك كفاية.
Script B — Variant 2
عمرك سألت نفسك ليه في ناس بتنجح في شغلها كل مرة وكأن الموضوع طبيعي بالنسبة لهم؟ الموضوع مش حظ. ومش إنهم أذكى. الفرق إنهم فاهمين نفسهم. عارفين إيه اللي بيحفزهم وإيه اللي بيطفيهم. وعارفين يختاروا البيئة اللي بتكبرهم.
Copy Strategy — What Was Done & Why
Hook mechanism
Both scripts open with a pattern interrupt that names a feeling the audience already has but hasn't articulated — triggering immediate identity recognition before any product mention.
Variant A logic
Opens with contrast: "not pressure — wrong direction." Targets audience who are already working hard but getting nowhere. Reframes the problem from effort to self-knowledge.
Variant B logic
Opens with a rhetorical question that seeds curiosity and mild envy. Removes luck and intelligence as variables — leaving self-awareness as the only differentiator. Positions the client as the solution to a specific gap.
CTA structure
Both close with reframing: not "buy a session" but "you might need a compass, not a new road." Lowers resistance by offering clarity instead of commitment.

Asset 02
Long-Form Script — Audience Retention Psychology
Client: ABZ Content (owned channel) · Platform: YouTube · Language: Arabic
اللحظة اللي المشاهد بيسحب فيها الفيديو بتاعك لفوق مش هي اللحظة اللي خد فيها القرار. القرار بيتاخد قبلها بثانيتين كاملين. الثانيتين دول هما أخطر مساحة زمنية في أي فيديو على يوتيوب. المشكلة إنك فاكر إن المشاهد بيشوفك كشخص طالع يتكلم معاه. الحقيقة هو بيشوفك كمجهود — عقله بيعمل حسبة رياضية في جزء من الثانية: هل اللي هشوفه هنا يستاهل إني أدفع من انتباهي؟
Script Architecture — Retention Engineering Applied
Opening hook
Opens with a counterintuitive reframe: the decision to leave happens 2 seconds before the swipe. Creates cognitive dissonance — the viewer thought they were in control. They weren't.
Information gap
Script deliberately withholds the "3-step system" until deep into the video — using named micro-promises ("the third step is the most dangerous") to sustain forward momentum without revealing the payoff early.
Self-referential structure
The script teaches retention mechanics while simultaneously applying them — the viewer experiences the framework as they learn it. This meta-layer dramatically increases perceived credibility and watch time.
Pattern interrupt timing
Breaks introduced every 20–30 seconds via tonal shifts, reframes, and direct audience address — matching the neurological attention reset window studied in behavioral psychology.

Performance Case — ABZ Content TikTok
Client work · Platform: TikTok · Role: Script + Content Strategy · ABZ Content
Case Breakdown
Problem
Client content was generating views but low save and share rates — indicating audiences were watching passively without perceiving the content as useful or worth returning to.
Decision
Restructured the script around high-utility information architecture: front-loaded the hook with a counterintuitive claim, embedded actionable frameworks mid-video, and closed with a callback that reinforced retention value.
Execution
Wrote and directed the content script for the ABZ Content TikTok channel — applying the same pacing and retention mechanics developed through 5+ years of music production to short-form video structure.
Outcome
Top-performing video on the channel to date. Save rate signals high-utility content — audiences bookmarking to return, not just consuming passively.
